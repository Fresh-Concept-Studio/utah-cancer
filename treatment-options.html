Treatment Options

Advanced, evidence-based therapies tailored to your specific cancer type, stage, and goals — with a focus on effectiveness and quality of life.

As a cancer patient, you are urged to take an active role in your care and make treatment decisions that are best for you and your loved ones. Our mission is to deliver compassionate, comprehensive, and patient-focused care using the latest treatment modalities.

medication

Chemotherapy

Systemic drug therapy that targets rapidly dividing cancer cells. Modern regimens are more effective and better tolerated than ever, with proactive side-effect management built into every plan.

radiology

Radiation Therapy

Precise, image-guided radiation using the TrueBeam Linear Accelerator. Treatments include IMRT, SBRT, stereotactic radiosurgery, and brachytherapy — delivered with millimeter accuracy.

vaccines

Immunotherapy

Therapies that harness your own immune system to recognize and attack cancer cells. Checkpoint inhibitors and other agents have transformed outcomes in many cancer types.

target

Targeted Therapy

Drugs designed to attack specific genetic mutations or proteins in cancer cells while sparing healthy tissue. Molecular profiling identifies which targets your cancer expresses.

balance

Hormone Therapy

Treatment that blocks or lowers hormones that fuel certain cancers, commonly used in breast and prostate cancer to slow or stop tumor growth.

science

Clinical Trials

Access to investigational treatments through 50+ active trials, including Utah’s only community-based early-phase program (START Mountain Region).

monitor_heart

Diagnostic Imaging

The Omni Legend Digital PET/CT scanner delivers AI-powered, low-dose imaging for precise diagnosis, staging, and treatment monitoring.

self_improvement

Supportive Care

Symptom management, pain control, nutrition counseling, and rehabilitative care — addressing the physical and emotional side effects of treatment.

bloodtype

Hematology

Expert diagnosis and treatment of blood cancers and benign blood disorders including anemia, clotting disorders, and bone marrow conditions.

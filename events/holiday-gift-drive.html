About this event

Treatment doesn't pause for back-to-school season. This drive is our way of taking one thing off the list for families in active care.

We're collecting backpacks, school supplies, and uniform basics for the kids of patients in active treatment. Drop off donations any time during the four-hour window, or stick around to help assemble bags for delivery. All ages welcome to volunteer — this is a great morning for kids and families.

What to expect

  • Drop-off any time in the lobby between 9 AM and 1 PM.
  • Volunteer to assemble stay 30 minutes or stay all morning.
  • Wish list provided see what's needed most this year.
  • Coffee & donuts for everyone who shows up.
  • Tax-deductible donations we'll send a receipt for any monetary giving.

Frequently asked

What's most needed?

Backpacks, lunchboxes, water bottles, notebooks, pencils, pens, glue sticks, scissors, and uniform basics. A wish list is posted on our website.

Can I donate money instead of supplies?

Yes — monetary donations let us fill exactly the gaps we see. Tax receipts provided.

Can my kids volunteer?

Yes! It's a kid-friendly morning. Younger volunteers should be accompanied by an adult.

Where do the bags go?

To children of patients currently in treatment at any UCS location.

Register

No registration needed. Drop in any time during open hours.

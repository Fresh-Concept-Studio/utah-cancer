TrueBeam™ Linear Accelerator

Pinpoint accuracy. Maximum precision.

The TrueBeam system enables a radically different approach to cancer treatment with image-guided radiotherapy. Developed from the ground up to deliver powerful cancer treatments with pinpoint accuracy, the system features a 120-leaf multileaf collimator that shapes the radiation beam to match the three-dimensional shape of each tumor.

Cone-beam CT capability reduces radiation exposure during imaging, while real-time imaging ensures targeting accuracy measured in millimeters. Gated respiration synchronization allows precise treatment of lung cancer and other motion-sensitive tumors.

Omni Legend Digital PET/CT

See more. Expose less.

The Omni Legend PET/CT scanner uses Precision DL (deep learning) image processing and an AI-based Auto Positioning Camera for ultra-fast scanning at lower radiation doses. Its industry-leading sensitivity per centimeter enables advanced small-lesion detection and low-contrast detectability.

Q-Clear image reconstruction with motion-free technology provides precise tumor measurement for confident diagnosis and treatment response monitoring.

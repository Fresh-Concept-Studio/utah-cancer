Our Mission

Choosing a path for your cancer treatment can be overwhelming. At Utah Cancer Specialists, our experienced team of physicians and caring professionals will help you make the best decisions for your journey — with the latest treatments, honest guidance, and a commitment to your whole well-being.

We believe cancer care means more than medications and therapies. As comprehensive cancer care providers, we address the emotional and physical impact of your disease and prepare you for a healthier life after treatment. We are united by a singular focus: helping every patient find a clear path to hope and healing.

Our Story

From a small group of dedicated oncologists to the Intermountain West's largest community cancer practice.

1994
Utah Cancer Specialists is founded by a small group of oncologists committed to bringing expert, community-based cancer care closer to where patients live and work along the Wasatch Front.
2000s
The practice expands to multiple locations, partnering with hospitals throughout the region, and launches its clinical research program to give patients access to emerging therapies.
2008
UCS opens its in-house oral oncology pharmacy, becoming one of the first community oncology practices in the region to offer medically integrated dispensing — ensuring patients receive medications with direct pharmacist-oncologist collaboration.
2016
The Jordan Valley Cancer Center opens — a purpose-built, two-story facility designed around patient comfort, with a hospitality-inspired environment featuring natural healing elements of earth, water, and wood.
2020
UCS becomes a founding site for the START Mountain Region early-phase clinical trial program — the only dedicated community-based early-phase trial center in Utah, led by Dr. Justin Call.
Today
With 18 locations spanning Utah, Idaho, and Wyoming, and more than 250 team members, UCS is the largest community-based oncology practice in the Intermountain West — offering medical oncology, radiation therapy, clinical trials, and integrated support services.

Cancer is not a solo journey

One of the benefits of community-based care is that you are never alone. Our multidisciplinary team provides a carefully coordinated treatment plan that draws on the wisdom, training, and experience of each individual team member — from medical oncologists and radiation specialists to nurse practitioners, pharmacists, and patient advocates.

Beyond clinical treatment, we offer educational classes, support groups, nutritional guidance, and gentle movement programs to help you and your family through every challenge.

Advancing the future of cancer treatment

Utah Cancer Specialists actively participates in clinical research, managing more than 50 active clinical trials at any given time. One of our greatest strengths is the ability to translate today's most promising laboratory findings into tomorrow's new, more effective treatments.

Through the START Mountain Region program, we offer Utah's only dedicated community-based early-phase clinical trial site. Patients benefit not only from treatments that target their cancer, but therapies designed to preserve quality of life during and after treatment.

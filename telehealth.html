Telehealth

Connecting with your physician through technology — virtual, home-based care without a disruption in treatment.

How It Works

Telehealth allows you to communicate with and be assessed by your physician or advanced care practitioner through your smartphone, tablet, or computer. The process is simple — your clinic will send you a clickable link, and you’re connected.

smartphone

Step 1: Receive Your Link

Your clinic sends a clickable link to your smartphone, tablet, or email before your appointment.

videocam

Step 2: Connect

Click the link at your scheduled time. No downloads or special software required.

stethoscope

Step 3: Your Visit

Meet face-to-face with your provider, discuss your care, and ask questions from the comfort of home.

Common Questions

What equipment do I need?

A smartphone, tablet, or computer with a camera and microphone. Most modern devices have everything you need built in.

Is telehealth covered by insurance?

Most insurance plans cover telehealth visits. Our patient advocates can verify your coverage before your appointment.

What types of visits can be done via telehealth?

Follow-up consultations, treatment discussions, symptom checks, lab result reviews, and survivorship visits are all well-suited for telehealth. Your care team will let you know if an in-person visit is needed.

Is my telehealth visit private and secure?

Yes. All telehealth visits use HIPAA-compliant, encrypted video connections to protect your privacy.

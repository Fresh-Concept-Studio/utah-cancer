Patient Portal

Utah Cancer Specialists offers secure, instant access to your Personal Health Record (PHR) through our Patient Portal. Your portal includes:

  • Your diagnosis and treatment information
  • Current medications and dosages
  • Lab results and imaging reports
  • Educational resources about your condition
  • Online support and discussion groups
  • Secure messaging with your care team

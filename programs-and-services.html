Programs & Services

Patient care that goes beyond medicine — addressing the physical, emotional, and practical challenges of cancer treatment.

Besides the physical effects of cancer and treatment, there are also the psychological and emotional effects that can be difficult to manage on your own. We offer a wide range of programs and services to our patients and their caregivers, and encourage you to incorporate stress reduction and community support into your daily self-care.

group

Support Groups

The Jordan Valley Cancer Center Support Group meets the first Wednesday of each month, 6–7 PM, facilitated by Dr. Gary Garner. Available online or in person at 3592 W. 9000 S., West Jordan.

self_improvement

SOMA Method Breathwork

Rhythmic and mindful breathing classes designed for cancer patients, held the second Thursday of each month at 7 PM (virtual). Led by Marie Asay.

volunteer_activism

Patient Advocacy

Dedicated advocates coordinate insurance, financial assistance, and emotional support from your very first visit through the completion of treatment.

restaurant

Nutrition Guidance

Nutritional support and education to help optimize your health during treatment and aid in recovery afterward.

healing

Supportive & Palliative Care

Symptom management, pain control, and quality-of-life support throughout your treatment journey, led by our supportive care physician Dr. Gary Garner.

school

Educational Classes

Group instruction covering topics including treatment expectations, managing side effects, caregiver support, and survivorship planning.

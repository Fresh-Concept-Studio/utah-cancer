About this clinic

Our Idaho Falls clinic, located at Eastern Idaho Regional Medical Center, extends UCS cancer care into southeastern Idaho. Patients in the Idaho Falls region can receive medical and radiation oncology treatment from board-certified UCS specialists without having to travel across state lines — with the same standard of care offered at all our Utah locations.

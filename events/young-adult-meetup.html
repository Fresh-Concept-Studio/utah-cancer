About this event

A diagnosis in your 20s or 30s comes with its own particular weight — career, relationships, fertility, finances. This group exists to talk about all of it.

Hosted by a young adult patient navigator (and several alumni who keep coming back), this meetup is intentionally informal. There's no agenda — just a circle of people in similar life stages making space for each other. We meet the first Tuesday of every month. Snacks on us.

What to expect

  • A peer group specifically for patients in their 20s and 30s.
  • No agenda the conversation goes where the room needs it to.
  • Confidentiality what's shared in the room stays in the room.
  • Coffee, tea & snacks always free.
  • Optional after-hangs many members grab dinner together after.

Frequently asked

How young is 'young adult'?

We use the medical definition: roughly 18–39. If you're close and the group sounds right, you're welcome.

Do I need to share?

No. Many people come and listen for months before saying much, and that's totally fine.

Can my partner come?

The first hour is patients-only. Partners are welcome for the optional after-hang.

Is it only for current patients?

Open to anyone diagnosed at any point in their 20s or 30s, including survivors.
